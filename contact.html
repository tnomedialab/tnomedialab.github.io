About us

TNO fosters groundbreaking research in a very wide range of domains such as energy, smart cities, optics, space, mobility, and telecommunications. In the MediaLab, we create innovative media technologies aimed at providing people with new and rich experiences, which they can enjoy wherever, whenever and with whomever they want. We continously seek to challenge ourselves with new research questions, from the market and academia, to remain at the forefront of technology breakthrough. We gladly welcome inquiries via email about possible collaborations with companies, universities and organisations.
